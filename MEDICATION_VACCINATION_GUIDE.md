## ‚ùì FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### Q1: Can I recover a deleted medication/vaccination?
**A:** No, deletions are permanent. Make sure you want to delete before confirming.

### Q2: Can I edit a medication/vaccination after adding it?
**A:** Currently, you need to delete and re-add. In future versions, edit feature will be available.

### Q3: Why can't my doctor see my added medications/vaccinations?
**A:** Your doctor can view all your records. They're sync data is updated when you save.

### Q4: Can I add medications for other people?
**A:** No, you can only manage your own health records for security reasons.

### Q5: What if I make a mistake when adding?
**A:** Simply delete it and add the correct information.
